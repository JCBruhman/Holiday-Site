About Justin.inc:

My company started all the way back in 2016 when I was a wee-lad, I was fond of learning and loved education, but as I'd look around me, people hated to learn anything. I was never able to understand why they did, regardless of the fact, I met up with someone from school in 2022, as I had witness what they had done with their life, I realized all that not paying attention has left him a smelly, poor bum with a job as a garbage man. I never hoped this would happened to anyone I knew, but sadly, that was only a dream, and this dream inspired me to have a new one. That dream was to make sure everyone one is able to get education they need and not end up a bum in life, this is the reason I've worked my ass off for this company.
